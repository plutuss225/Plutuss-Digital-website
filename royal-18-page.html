ABOUT PROJECT

Smart Homes for
Modern Living

Royal 18 by Shree Anand Vyankateshwara Builders is a thoughtfully designed residential project located in Thergaon, Pune. Offering well-planned 1 BHK and 2 BHK apartments, the project is ideal for individuals and families seeking comfort, connectivity, and value.

Spread across 0.149 acres, the project includes 1 building with modern infrastructure and smart layouts. The homes are designed for efficient space utilization with unit sizes starting from 302 sq.ft.

Royal 18 is RERA registered (P52100018047) and ensures a convenient lifestyle with essential amenities and excellent connectivity to major parts of Pune.
