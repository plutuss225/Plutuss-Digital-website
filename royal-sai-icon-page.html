ABOUT THE PROJECT

A Perfect Blend of
Comfort & Convenience

Royal Sai Icon by Sai Krupa Builders is a thoughtfully designed residential project located in Thergaon, Pune. Offering ready-to-move 1 BHK and 2 BHK apartments, the project combines modern architecture with practical layouts for everyday living.

The project features well-planned units ranging from 582 to 980 sq.ft., ensuring efficient space utilization. Designed to meet the needs of urban families, Royal Sai Icon provides a balanced lifestyle with essential amenities and a secure gated environment.

With excellent connectivity and strong social infrastructure including schools, hospitals, and banks nearby, it offers both comfort and long-term value for homeowners and investors.
