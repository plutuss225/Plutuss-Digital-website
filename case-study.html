Empowering Clean Energy with a Powerful Digital Identity

Revamping Prosumers Solar’s online presence through a modern website, strong content, and social media strategy to drive engagement and lead generation.

Overview

Prosumers Solar is a rising player in the renewable energy sector, aiming to educate consumers and grow its client base. Their challenge was limited digital visibility, weak social engagement, and a lack of a structured platform to communicate their value proposition.

Project Objectives

  • Build a professional, SEO-friendlywebsite to improve online credibility
  • Develop educational and conversion-driven content
  • Establish consistent and strategic social media presence
  • Drive quality inquiries through digital marketing

Case Process — Prosumers

Our four-step approach to revamp Prosumers’ digital identity and boost engagement.

01

Audit & Discovery

Assessed Prosumers' existing digital footprint, competitor landscape, and customer behavior.

Goal: Understand current gaps and opportunities in digital presence.

02

Website Design & Content Planning

Created a clean, responsive website with simplified user navigation and high-impact messaging.

Goal: Improve user experience and brand perception.

03

Social Media Strategy & Execution

Built monthly content calendars, created branded visuals, and managed platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

Goal: Strengthen brand awareness and connect with the right audience.

04

Analytics & Optimization

Integrated performance tracking tools and ran iterative improvements on social and web assets based on audience data.

Goal: Continuously refine strategies for higher engagement and conversions.

Results

  • 3X increase in website engagement within the first 60 days
  • 5X growth in social media impressions and post interactions
  • Increase in inbound inquiries from potential residential and commercial clients
  • Positive brand recognition in a competitive green energy market
