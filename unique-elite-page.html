ABOUT THE PROJECT

Modern Living with Smart Design

Unique Elite by Unique Group is a thoughtfully planned residential development located in Chinchwad, Pune. Designed for modern families, it offers well-crafted 2 & 3 BHK apartments with efficient layouts and contemporary design.

Spread across 0.26 acres, the project features 2 buildings with a limited number of 20 units, ensuring privacy and exclusivity.

With excellent connectivity through Spine Road and proximity to schools, hospitals, and shopping destinations, Unique Elite offers a balanced lifestyle for both end-users and investors.
